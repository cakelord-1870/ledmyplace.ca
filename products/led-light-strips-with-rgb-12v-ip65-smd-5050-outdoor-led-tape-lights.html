Outdoor RGB LED Strip Lights, 12V, IP65, SMD 5050, Waterproof Multicolor Strip Lights, For cabinets, under-cabinets, windows

The dimmable outdoor LED strip lights are the perfect installation for creating ambiance outdoors as it is waterproof. It requires 12V for getting operated and delivers an output of 94 Lumens. It is also known as weatherproof strip lights as it is IP65 rated, which can resist both dirt and water. With an 8mm PCB, the product can be installed with a 3M adhesive tape. It’s a versatile outdoor LED strip light that can brighten both settings, whether indoor or outdoor, including cabinets, under-cabinets, windows, patios, landscapes, vehicles, etc.

Easy Installation

The 12 Volt LED Strip lights are decorative lights best suited for indoor applications. These strip light fixtures are easy to install using a premium 3M Sticky.  The 3M tape is an adhesive tape that helps establish a connection and install the LED strip light. For a complete step-by-step guide, refer to the user guide (inside the package).

50,000 Life Hours

LED Strip Lights come with a tested lifespan of 50,000 hours or 5.7 years, making them durable and pocket-friendly.

Adhesive Backing

The LED Strip Lights come with the 3M adhesive tape that is very helpful while installing. All you need to do is to remove the tape and stick it to the area you want.

Cut into Strip 

LED Strip Lights are supposed to cut from a particular place; otherwise, it can affect the entire LED Light strip. The LED Strips Light soldier has the indication defining the right area to cut the Light strip. After cutting the strip light, connect it with the connector.

Plug and Play

This LED strip light fixture comes with a direct plug and play-installation that enables you to connect these strips directly or with a connecting cable (inside the package), facilitating easy installation.

High Beam Angle

The beam angle of these lights is 180 degrees, considered a high beam angle. It helps in rendering Light in a large area.

Flexible

This 60 LED/m LED strip light is a flexible strip light that can be powered directly from your outlet and is designed for easy installation.
