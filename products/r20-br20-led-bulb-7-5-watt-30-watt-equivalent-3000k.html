3000 Kelvin - LED Bulb BR20, 7.5 Watt - 30W Equivalent, 525 Lumens - Dimmable UL, Energy Star Approved

LED Light bulbs are standard among interior designers and home decorators. These bulbs are mostly used in showrooms, retail stores, and track lights, etc. These 7.5W LED Light Bulbs come in several varieties with a CCT of 3000K. These light fixtures are designed to sustain and function in a voltage ranging between 120-277V. With a lumen output of 525 Lumens, these LED Light Bulb Fixtures can easily replace 30W fluorescents. It's a UL and Energy Star certified light fixture that comes with a lifespan of 15,000 hours and a manufacturer warranty of 3-years. This LED Light Bulb lighting fixture is a modern lighting fixture that is highly durable and very cost-effective.

Instant On

These lights do not take much time to get switched on. These bulbs have higher operational efficiency and do not need any warming up or cooling time.

E26 Base

Because of its durability, the fixture comes with an E26 base, which is one of the most frequently used bases frequently known as the industry standard for all lighting fixtures. Any bulb with an E26 base can be used in these easily.

High CRI

The CRI of LED bulbs is greater than 80. Color rendering index (CRI) refers to the ability to display correct colors. A high color rendering illumination can enhance the vibrancy and color of any scene. It has the potential to improve the appearance of your home by revealing the true colors of objects up to 90%.

UV & Mercury Free

As the world has become more environmentally aware, people have become enamored of oil and environmentally friendly products. Installing LED Light bulbs not only saves energy and money but also encourages a cleaner atmosphere because our LED Light bulbs are free of toxic chemicals like mercury, cadmium, and lead.

 

Flicker Free

Until the end of their lifetime, LED light bulbs provide flicker-free illumination. These lights use very little energy during initialization, ensuring long-term longevity.

No Dark Spot

All of our bulbs, which use the most up-to-date solid-state lighting technology, are premium quality bulbs that, when mounted in any location, can virtually remove all dark spots, ensuring that your space is well-lit at all times.

Dimmable

Our bulbs being dimmable have the capacity to dim down from 10 volts to 1 volts, resulting in further energy-savings on top of what you achieve by switching from any conventional light bulb to our LED bulbs.

 

Rust Free

Our bulbs come with bases that are completely rust-free and so it's easy to take out and screw back in, whenever you want to. 

Rebate Eligible

The LED Bulbs being Energy Star approved comes within the scope of rebate-eligibility criteria and hence you can be eligible for rebate.
